Millions of kids are struggling in the aftermath of Typhoon Haiyan. Together, we can help them rebuild their lives.

OrphanAid is there to provide food, shelter and other lifesaving essentials to young survivors of the storm. The UN says 1.5 million children are facing malnutrition as the Philippines struggles to recover from Super Typhoon Haiyan.

  • 13 million people in 44 provinces, 539 municipalities and 56 cities have been affected by the typhoon
  • The death toll has reached 5,260
  • The storm surge reached a height of 17 feet in Tacloban, where some of the worst flooding was recorded
